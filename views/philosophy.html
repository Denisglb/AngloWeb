Our Philosophy

The educational system of the United Kingdom can seem daunting to those unfamiliar with its particulars, yet to this day remains a global exemplar without peer or compare. Our long-standing expertise enables us to provide both our clients and their children with an unparalleled foundation upon which the greatest of academic success that can be aspired to is placed within reach. Our industry-leading consultants assist our clients in choosing the best independent (private) school for their child most suited to their character and ability and guide our more senior students through the applications processes of the United Kingdom’s leading universities. Through our bespoke and industry-leading careers development programme, we further instil in every student of ours the skills needed to succeed at interview upon graduation in pursuit of the highest professional echelons of their chosen vocations. Our careers advisory service offers unrivalled preparation for your child from the moment they enter the academic rigours of university life to their introduction into the demands of the working world.


AngloSlav Education is dedicated to managing the needs of your child throughout the duration of their studies, offering parents peace of mind and security through the provision of a service rooted in consummate expertise and peerless professionalism. Our consultancy offers a total-care package from the first day of a student’s stay in the United Kingdom and throughout their study period, acting as our clients’ personal representative and speaking on their behalf in all matters concerning their child to ensure their constant wellbeing and continued success.

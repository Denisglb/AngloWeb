Our Vision

At AngloSlav, our vision is to nurture the knowledge and talents of a future generation of global leaders. We are committed to securing the lifelong professional success of our students by helping them to reach a plain of unparalleled academic excellence and achievement by which their passions may too be enduringly bound to their chosen professional arena.

Our Mission

We help to shape young people into innovative, resourceful and charismatic individuals, confident in their own talents and capabilities. Our service is able to help secure for the children of our clients, placements within the most prestigious educational institutions of the United Kingdom and further enhance their profiles towards becoming those candidates, continuously sought by leading global firms.

Our Values

  • We are esteemed by our clients for our professionalism as well as our extensive knowledge, matchless insights and pioneering techniques within the field of educational consultancy.

  • We pride ourselves on being a warm and welcoming extended family to our clients, always capable of cultivating the greatest of virtues in all of our students.

  • At AngloSlav, we remain steadfast in the preservation of the highest professional standards and the maintenance of our clients’ confidentiality therein.

"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world."

Nelson Mandela

The United Kingdom remains home to some of the world’s most renowned universities. Oxford, Cambridge, Imperial College London, UCL - each of these institutions, amongst others, speak of the eminence and distinction of the British Educational system, providing both domestic and international students with access to world-class teaching and research facilities.

Yet, whilst the United Kingdom’s educational system is well-known for its academic excellence and duly revered as such across the globe, it is within the employable value that a degree obtained within the United Kingdom presents and its demand amongst leading global firms, that positions graduates of British universities amidst the most sought-after and highly successful within the international postgraduate arena. With a rich and profound history and culture, commanding economic status, and peerless international standing, the elite schools and universities of the United Kingdom remain the perfect environment by which students may gain a competitive edge and become the leaders of tomorrow.

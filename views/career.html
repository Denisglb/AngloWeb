Career Guidance

Since the point of our inception over 20 years ago, AngloSlav Education has helped, assisted and nurtured hundreds of students from countries as far afield as Russia, Ukraine, Belarus, Kazakhstan, France, Italy, Germany, Holland and Israel, to name but a few. We take enormous pride in our innovative and industry-leading services that, more than being steeped in consummate dedication and professionalism, definitively reflect our enduring belief that in each student, a unique and manifest greatness waits to be uncovered and fulfilled. From the moment our clients entrust us with their children, each benefits from a bespoke programme created in consultation with both parent and child and which befits the impeccable standard to which we are both recognised and to which we ourselves are held.

The personalised support that each of our students receive is complemented by an insight and expertise that has been established over years of experience and which remains sustained by our dedicated team whose tireless professionalism remains underpinned by the all-encompassing care that we give to each of our students, helping them to feel at home, nurturing their talents, bringing out their best qualities and laying a foundation for a future of immutable esteem. We welcome enquiries from across the world and provide each of our clients with an exclusive, discreet and accomplished service, highly-specialised in the following fields: Guardianship, School and University Entrance, Career Planning, Academic Coaching and Tuition, and Personal Development.

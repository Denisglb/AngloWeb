Guardianship Services

For over twenty years our firm has remained at the forefront of premier guardianship services having cared for countless children since our inception.



Guardianship

Our consultancy works in the professional provision of guardianship services to international students from UHNW families attending elite boarding schools and universities throughout the United Kingdom. We provide these services to children whose parents live abroad, working tirelessly for both to ensure that our students attain the greatest of success at their chosen schools and universities. It remains a legal requirement in the United Kingdom for all international students below and of 18 years of age and whose parents remain resident abroad to possess a guardian based in the United Kingdom upon commencement of their studies. Our consultancy works in the very provision of such guardianship services, acting as a personal and devoted guardian to each of our students whilst never hiring or outsourcing our services to any external representative - individual or institutional.

When parents elect for their children to study in the United Kingdom, they not only afford their child an opportunity to study within the world’s leading educational institutions, but also to obtain a traditional up-bringing. Yet, whilst this unique experience remains a life affirming journey of incomparable merit, it is not without its complications, and it is here wherein the invaluable support offered by our guardianship services enter into their own.

To enjoy the greatest benefits that a British education is able to offer, it is vital that the process remain consistently positive and devoid of all difficulty and hindrance. At AngloSlav, we pride ourselves on offering a guardianship service of unparalleled diligence and devotion to ensure that every need, no matter how small or seemingly unimportant, is always meticulously attended to. The happiness and wellbeing of our clients’ children is of the utmost importance to us and the consistent fulfilment of their needs remains our cardinal concern. We provide parents peace of mind and security in being of source of steadfast assistance and guidance.


Our consultancy provides a full range of services for the children of our clients from the moment they arrive in the United Kingdom until the end of their study period and beyond. We understand that during your child’s attendance of boarding school, knowing that they are continuously being cared for during term time, the weekends, half term or holidays by a trusted guardian remains a matter of paramount importance, the significance of which cannot be overstated.

In tandem with the ever-changing pace of the world, we continue to adapt to the expanding needs of our clients and offer a range of additional services that have been honed over the two decades of our professional experience:

  •   Confidence and Performance Coaching
  •   Assistance with Personal Statements
  •   Assistance with Job Applications
  •   Interview Preparation
  •   Life Coaching
  •   Motivational Training
  •   Preparation for University Entry and Admission
  •   Enhanced technological literacy


Our services are always expanding and we ceaselessly strive to cater to each of our clients’ requests, however particular.
